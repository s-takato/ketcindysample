Basel Problem

The next inequality was derived by Prof.Oshima.

 $A_m=\displaystyle\pi\theta_m=\sum_{k=1}^m\frac{1}{k^2}$

 $B_m=\pi\theta_m+\dfrac{1}{m+\tfrac{1}{2}}$, $C_m=\pi \theta_m + \dfrac{1}{m+\tfrac{1}{2}}-\dfrac{1}{12(m^2-\tfrac{1}{4})(m+\tfrac{3}{2})}$

 $\Longrightarrow C_m < A_{\infty} < B_m$
