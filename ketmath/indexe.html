KeTMath     2022-08-02

KeTMath is a tool to send/receive math expressions in text format based on $\TeX$.
For example, the left side below is the notation of KeTMath for the $\TeX$ expression on the right side.
  fr(1,3)sin(3x) ＝＞$\frac{1}{3}\sin 3x$
